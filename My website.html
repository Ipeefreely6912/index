Home made banana bread with chocolate chips.

There's a lot of different banana bread recipes on the web, I personally have roughly four different recipes for everyone in my life. My dad doesn't like his banana bread with chocolate chips, so I'll be adding in that one to this website as well. But I found this one is not too moist or too dry, it's the perfect blend of both. When you cut it, it doesn't squish or crumble, it hold's it's shape even when you freeze them.

Ingredients

Here's a list of ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

You will need three separate bowls for this next part

  1. Preheat your oven to 325 degrees farenheit, grease a 9 by 5 inch baking pan
  2. Mix together your flour, baking podwer, baking soda and salt in one bowl.
  3. Stir together your banana's, milk and cinnamon in a separate bowl.
  4. Beat together your butter and sugar in your third bowl until light and fluffy. Add your 2 eggs into your butter mixture one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Stir banana mix into your butter mixture.
  6. Stir in your flour mixture into your banana and butter mixture, until well blended.
  7. Fold in chocolate chips until combined.
  8. Pour batter into your pan.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until a tooth pick inserted in the center comes out clean, roughly 70 minutes.
  10. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing from the pan and cool completely
